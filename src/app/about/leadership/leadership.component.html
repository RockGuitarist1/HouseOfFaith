Conrade & Lisa Duncan

Senior Pastors

In the late 70s we had meetings in the barn all the time, bible studies, fellowship meetings, worship nights; the horses would be in the stall down below and the people would be in the loft, sitting on bales of hay, and no one would go home – they’d stay into the wee hours of the night with the fog rolling in across the fields - In 1990, I heard the Lord tell me to prepare the barn for year round ministry.
