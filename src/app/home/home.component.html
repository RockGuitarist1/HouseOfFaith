Our Church

Our call is to equip and empower others to find their gifts and calling in Christ. Our commitment is to seeing the Church united without walls or denominational barriers. Our mission is to demonstrate the Gospel through an active love for others. Our outreach is recognizing the need and mobilizing to meet it. If you are looking for a place that is grace based, welcoming believers from all walks of life, we invite you to join us.

Outreach

Mission Outreach is defined by Wikipedia as "an activity of providing services to those who might not otherwise have access to those services. A key component of outreach is that the groups providing it are not stationary, but mobile". This means we go to the needy; we set up shop where the hardship exists: loving and lifting, delivering services, educating and training, striving to be a change agent in the midst of the community.
